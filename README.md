# YouthCare+

YouthCare+ is an inclusive digital health platform designed to provide youth in Rwanda with access to reliable sexual, reproductive, and mental health support.

## What YouthCare+ Offers

- **AI-powered chatbot** for private, judgment-free health questions
- **Menstrual cycle tracker** with personalized reminders and notifications
- **Mental health support** with therapist guidance concepts
- **Educational resources** including health videos and articles

## Accessibility by Design

YouthCare+ is built for broad accessibility across multiple channels:

- **Web platform**
- **USSD (simulated) support**
- **Call support concepts**

This multi-channel approach helps ensure support is available even for users without smartphones or stable internet access.

## Vision

YouthCare+ aims to empower young people with accurate information, improve health outcomes, and create a safe, supportive environment for all users.
